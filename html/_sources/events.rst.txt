Events
================================
Occassionally we host events for people to experience and learn about VR.


VR Scene Experience West Ryde 
----------------------------------
On **21/Dec/2025** from 11:45 AM to 1:30 PM, we are hosting a free event with the West Ryde Anglican Church, with some VR scenes experience. The VR scenes are from these Apps: Open Brush, Gravity Sketch, and Theatre Elsewhere. 




Scenes
-------------------------
**Summer Canal** : Several old houses at the bank of the canal. Trees around them.

**Hover Bike** : A girl riding a hover bike

**Space X Mars Colony**: 	An astronaut holding a flag outside a landing pod on Mars

**Old City**: 	B&W houses with stairs in an old city block
	
**Desperado Scene**: A man and a woman are walking on a rooftop, with guns in hand

**Lunch Break**: 	Skyscraper builders having lunch on a steel bar

**Xmas Snowball**: 	Snow falling onto a Christmas tree, in a glass ball

**An afternoon in Tokyo**: A boy meets a girl at stairs

**Mother of dragons**: 	A lady is riding a flying dragon, and the dragon is spitting fire.

**Rusty Spaceship**: Several space ships

**A Lone Ranger**: 	A cowboy and an American Indian are riding horses going across the plains
