Events
================================
Occassionally we host events for people to experience and learn about VR.


VR Scene Experience West Ryde 
----------------------------------
On **21/Dec/2025** from 11:45 AM to 1:30 PM, we hosted a free event with the West Ryde Anglican Church, with some VR scenes experience. The VR scenes are from these Apps: Open Brush and Theatre Elsewhere.
