Holiday Programs
=================
From Dec/2025 to Jan/2025, we have some programs suitable for families, childen and adults.


VR Party Hire 
--------------------
.. image:: _static/images/mars1.png
   :align: center


Hire 2 or 3 VR headsets, and let kids or adults to have fun in VR !

1. Experience beautiful and inspiring VR scenes/objects created by artists

2. Watch short VR films

3. Create objects, scenes or buildings in VR using the famous tilt brush! Decorate your own living room (single floor, single room) using special effects like star, snow, rainbow, etc! 

4. Play interactive VR games, single player or multi-player. Extra cost apply depends on games.


**Pricing** 
 
- 2 headsets for $90 for 1 hour, $150 for 2 hours 
- 3  headsets for 120 for 1 hour, $220 for 2 hours.

We are located at Ryde area. Extra fees ($10-$20) apply if far from Ryde. 


VR Creative Camp 
--------------------

.. image:: _static/images/OpenBrush1.jpeg
   :align: center

.. image:: _static/images/OpenBrush2.jpeg
   :align: center


Come and learn to use Open Brush/Tilt Brush for 2 hours. 2-3 students at least. 

- 30 minutes of tutorial time
- 50 minutes of practice time
- 20-30 minutes to experience other artists works

Location at Marsfield, NSW 2122. 

**Pricing** 

$100 per person, at least two persons in a group. 

Contact: **0478266266**  by SMS or WhatsApp.
